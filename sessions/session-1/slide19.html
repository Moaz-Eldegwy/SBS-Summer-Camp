Data — the raw material

To learn the patterns of human language,
you need a mind-bending amount of human language.

We happened to spend twenty years pouring the entire internet — articles, forums, books, code, images — into one place. For the first time in history, there was enough raw material to learn from.

20 yrs
of everything humanity typed, uploaded and argued about —
stacked up and ready, right when we needed it
