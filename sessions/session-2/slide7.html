These pieces are called tokens.
Think Lego bricks

The model doesn’t see a smooth stream of words. It sees a box of standard bricks it already knows how to handle.

Sometimes a word, sometimes a piece

“cat” is one whole token. “understanding” is three. The split isn’t about grammar — it’s about what shows up often.

Why chunks and not whole words?

With a few thousand reusable pieces it can build any word, in any language — even ones it has never seen before.
